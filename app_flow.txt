Authentication
    Registration:

    /register (POST): Accepts username, email, password. Validates and stores credentials securely using bcrypt.
    Login:

    /login (POST): Verifies credentials, sets a session cookie with an expiration.
    Logout:

    /logout (GET): Invalidates the session cookie.

Post Management
    Create Post:

    /posts/create (POST): Allows logged-in users to create posts with a title, content, and category.
    View Post:

    /posts/:id (GET): Displays a post with its comments count and like/dislike counts.
    List Posts:

    /posts (GET): Displays all posts with filters (e.g., by category).

Commenting
    Add Comment:

    /comments/add (POST): Allows logged-in users to add comments to a post.
    View Comments:

    /posts/:id (GET): Part of the post view, shows associated comments.

Likes/Dislikes
    Like/Dislike Post or Comment:
    /like or /dislike (POST): Allows users to like or dislike a post or comment.
